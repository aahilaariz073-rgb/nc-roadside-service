/ COVERAGE — PINEVILLE, NC

Roadside assistance in Pineville, Mecklenburg County.

Pineville sits at the crossroads of south Charlotte and Mecklenburg County's southern border — a dense commercial zone where Johnston Road (US-521), NC-51, and the I-485 southern interchange push enormous daily traffic. Retail workers, shoppers at Carolina Place, and commuters all share these roads, and when something goes wrong, the call comes to us.

NC Roadside Service's coverage of Pineville includes all of the Carolina Place Mall area, the Johnston Road commercial corridor, and the residential neighborhoods east and west of US-521. We handle everything from parking lot lockouts to highway shoulder jump starts.

The proximity to the South Carolina border means we also see calls from drivers who started their trip in Rock Hill or Fort Mill and broke down just north of the state line. We cover that zone too.
